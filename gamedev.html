Cannon Climbing


Cannon Climbing is a challenging and addictive precision platformer where you play as a cannon on a quest to climb to the top of a giant test chamber.
Use your shooting abilities to propel yourself upwards,
but be careful - one wrong move and you'll fall all the way back down to the bottom.
